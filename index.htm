The lemur

Lemurs are characterized by large eyes, a small head, and a long tail. Their appearance and fur color vary among different species. Lemurs live in trees and are adapted to thrive in Madagascar's diverse environments, including forests, savannas, and wetlands.

Koala information

  • The koala (Phascolarctos cinereus) is a marsupial native to Australia and is known for its distinctive appearance, tree-dwelling behavior, and diet of eucalyptus leaves.
  • Koalas have a stout, round body covered in dense, woolly fur that helps them regulate their body temperature.
  • They have a large head with fluffy ears, a prominent black nose, and small eyes.
